Core coach agents

Three agents to run your practice

1) Matchmaker & Triage

Classifies client goals, parses uploads (PDFs, labs, notes), flags risks, and routes to the best coach or queue.

2) Program Architect

Turns goals into structured programs with milestones and KPIs. Adapts based on adherence and feedback.

3) Practice Ops

Automates CRM, scheduling, billing, reminders, and risk alerts—so coaches can coach, not admin.
